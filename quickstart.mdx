---
title: "Quickstart: your first gated study"
description: "Get a verification check in front of your survey in three steps, so only verified, unique humans reach your paid questions."
---

VerifyYou wraps any survey with a ~20-second liveness and uniqueness check. The respondent completes the check, receives a token, and your survey or platform validates that token before counting a response. This page walks you through the three steps to get that working.

<Note>
The strength of token enforcement in step 3 depends on which survey tool you use. Some platforms let you block entry before a single question loads; others validate the instant a respondent submits. See [Platform tiers](/platform-tiers) and [Survey tool overview](/survey/overview) to pick the right approach for your study.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a verification in the dashboard">
    Log into the VerifyYou dashboard and create a new Verification. Give it a name and paste your survey URL as the destination. The dashboard generates a gated link:

    ```
    app.verifyyou.com/verification/ID
    ```

    That gated link is what you share with respondents. Your raw survey URL stays private. When a respondent opens the gated link, they complete the liveness and uniqueness check on our hosted page. On success, we forward them to your survey with `?vyt=TOKEN&vyc=1` appended to the URL. The token is a single-use claim ticket we minted against one unique face; it only matches if we created it, and we only create one per person.

    You can also configure a Success Redirect URL here if you want to control exactly where verified respondents land.

    <Tip>
      If you need to carry a participant identifier (for example a Prolific ID or a panel RID) through the check and back onto your survey URL, use the `pass_params` option when creating the verification via the API. See [POST /v3/initialize](https://docs.verifyyou.com) for details.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Put the gated link where your respondents start">
    Replace any raw survey URL in your recruitment flow with the gated link from step 1. Where exactly you paste it depends on your recruitment platform:

    | Platform | Where to put the gated link |
    |---|---|
    | Prolific | Study URL field |
    | MTurk / CloudResearch | External HIT URL |
    | Cint / Lucid | Entry point URL |
    | Your own list | The link you send in email or SMS |

    From the respondent's point of view: they click, they see a brief liveness check, and then they land on your survey. From your point of view: anyone who reaches your survey has already passed our check.

    <Note>
      A failure or screen-out redirect (to send rejected respondents back to the panel as a disqualification) is not yet available in the dashboard. It is on the build list. In the meantime, respondents who do not pass the check see an end-of-session message on our hosted page.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add the token check on your survey">
    The token arrives on your survey URL as `?vyt=TOKEN`. One setup step in your survey tool wires up validation so a bad or missing token is caught before it becomes a paid, counted response.

    What that step looks like depends on your tool:

    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="True gate" icon="shield-check">
        Checked before any question loads. No bypass possible. Works with Qualtrics personal links, Alchemer login credentials, MTurk qualifications, or a researcher-hosted wrapper page. Best choice for any sensitive or incentivized study.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Soft gate + webhook" icon="circle-xmark">
        Checked the instant a respondent submits. Bad responses are auto-deleted via the platform API before they reach your CSV. Works with Typeform, Jotform, Tally, SurveyMonkey, and Google Forms.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>

    For either path, the validation call is a single HTTP request to our endpoint using a per-Flow publishable validation key (`pk_val_...`):

    ```http
    GET /v3/survey/peek?vyt=TOKEN
    Authorization: Bearer pk_val_FLOW_RAND
    ```

    This returns `{ verified, reason }` without burning the token, so you can check it at the gate and again from a webhook without any conflict. When you are ready to accept a completion as final, a second call consumes the token:

    ```http
    POST /v3/survey/consume?vyt=TOKEN
    Authorization: Bearer pk_val_FLOW_RAND
    ```

    That returns `{ consumed }` and marks the token used. It cannot be replayed.

    <Warning>
      Always use a `pk_val_` validation key for survey-side checks. Never expose your `sk_` secret key in a survey tool credential store, and never call the endpoint without a key. An open endpoint enables token-burn attacks and lets anyone probe whether a token exists.
    </Warning>

    Step-by-step setup guides for each platform are in [Survey tool overview](/survey/overview).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What you get

Once all three steps are in place, the flow looks like this:

1. A respondent clicks your recruitment link and lands on the VerifyYou check.
2. They complete a ~20-second liveness selfie. We check liveness and uniqueness.
3. If they pass, we forward them to your survey with `?vyt=TOKEN&vyc=1`.
4. Your survey validates the token. A bad or missing token is caught before any question is answered (true gate) or before the response is saved (soft gate + webhook).
5. Only verified, unique humans reach your paid questions.

The uniqueness check is what closes the loop on repeat takers: the same person cannot earn a second token, so even if they share a link or try again, they never get another valid ticket. That is the property that matters most for data quality in high-incentive studies.
